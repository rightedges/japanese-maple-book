# Siting and Planting

## Sun vs. Shade

While some cultivars tolerate full sun, most prefer afternoon shade.

## Planting steps

1. Dig a wide hole, not too deep.
2. Tease out the roots.
3. Place the tree so the root flare is slightly above grade.
4. Backfill with native soil.
