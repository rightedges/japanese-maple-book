## Overview
'Kuro hime' (translating to "Black-Red Princess") is a captivating dwarf variety known for its intense spring color and compact habit. Ideally suited for small gardens and containers, it offers big impact in a small package.

## Key Features & Seasonal Interest
- **Spring**: Emerges with spectacular reddish-pink leaves that darken to a deep purple-red, giving it its "Black-Red" name.
- **Summer**: Foliage transitions to a pleasing green with red margins, maintaining interest throughout the warm months.
- **Fall**: Putting on a final show, the leaves turn a brilliant bright red.

## Garden Use & Care
Its slow growth and dense branching make it perfect for bonsai culture, rock gardens, or as a focal point in a patio container.

### Maintenance Tips
- **Pruning**: Minimal pruning is required. Remove any dead wood and lightly shape to maintain its mounding form.
- **Location**: Prefers morning sun and afternoon shade to protect its delicate foliage from scorching.
- **Watering**: Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged; ensuring good drainage is key.
