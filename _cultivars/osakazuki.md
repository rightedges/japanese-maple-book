## Overview
Widely considered to have the best fall color of any Japanese Maple. 'Osakazuki' is a vigorous, large-growing tree that stays a handsome green for most of the year before turning an intense, fire-engine red in autumn.

## Care Tips
- **Fall Display:** To maximize the scarlet fall display, ensure the tree receives adequate summer water.
- **Size:** This is a large variety; ensure it has plenty of space to reach its 20+ foot maturity.
- **Sun:** Very sun-tolerant compared to other green varieties.
